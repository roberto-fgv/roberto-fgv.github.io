About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Business Administration at Fundação Getúlio Vargas (FGV EAESP), focusing on Scalable Organizational Agility: Team Adaptability, Self-Organization, and Continuous Improvement. With extensive experience in academic administration and teaching assistance, I have worked with several prestigious universities in Brazil and abroad.

My research interests include information systems, business process management, and organizational agility. I am passionate about education and constantly seeking to improve administrative processes in academic environments.
